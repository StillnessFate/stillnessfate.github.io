How to use NewSTyle (User Manual)


View Card News

You can click on the title of each card news to view its contents.

This can be done by anyone.


Register and Login

You must log in first to create and edit your card news.

You can log in by clicking the [Login] button on the upper right. If you do not have an account, you can create a new account by pressing the [Register] button.


Create New Card News

You can create a new card by pressing the [New Card News] button on the bottom right corner. In the cover image, enter the URL of the image file.

This is only possible with login.


Refresh Card News List

Reload the card news list.


Edit or Delete Card News

You can edit or delete card news via the [Edit] and [Delete] buttons of each card news.

This is only possible for authors of card news.


Edit Card News Info

You can change the basic information of the card news by pressing the [Edit card news info] button.

This is only possible for authors of card news.


Edit Card News Pages

You can add or modify pages of the card news by pressing the [Edit card news pages] button.

This is only possible for authors of card news.


New Card News Page

Create a new card news page.

This is only possible for authors of card news.


Edit Card News Page

Edit card news page.

Use the editor to edit the card news page.

This is only possible for authors of card news.


Delete Card News Page

Delete the card news page.

Use the editor to edit the card news page.

This is only possible for authors of card news.


Share to SNS

You can share your card news to the selected SNS by pressing the share button of each card news.
